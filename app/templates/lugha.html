Nairobi Slang Basics

Learn the language that describes the people.

Greetings

  • "Niaje?"- What's up?

    • Simple response:"Niko fiti" - I'm cool.

Directions

  • "Nionyeshe njia ya kwenda Giraffe Centre"- Give me directions to Giraffe Center.
  • "Hapa ni wapi"-- Where is this?
  • "Naweza nunua chakula wapi?"- Where can I buy food?

Currency

  • Pesa- Money

    • Amounts

  • "mbao" - Twenty Shillings
  • "chwani" - Fifty Shillings
  • "soo" - One hundres Shillings
  • "rwabe" - Two Hundred Shillings
  • "punch" - Five Hundred Shillings
  • "thao" - One thousand shillings
